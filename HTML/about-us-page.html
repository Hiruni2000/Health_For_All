About Us



Based on a web assignment, it was started by a group of 21.1 students at NSBM Green University. Thousands of schools across Sri Lanka select the schools with the lowest health facilities based on specific criteria and improve the health facilities and related infrastructure of those schools.

This website is designed to contact people who can make the necessary financial or other contributions. We look forward to working with our university branch at the leo club, a voluntary club throughout Sri Lanka.

Who we are




We are a collective group of volunteers who are happy to colloborate with others to make a safe and healthy world to the children in low facilitated schools.
